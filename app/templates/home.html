FaceSense & TempTrank

FaceSense Attendance monitoring system with temperature is a facial recognition and temperature monitoring system that combines the power of face recognition with the accuracy of thermal temperature screening. It can be used to identify and verify people, as well as to measure and record their temperature. This information can be used to track attendance, identify potential health risks, and ensure the safety of employees and visitors.
